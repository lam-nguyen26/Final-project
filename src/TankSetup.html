Tank Setup

Let's learn more about shrimps and aquariums

Tank shape

- A longer tank rather than a tall one provides more surface area for shrimps to graze and explore.

Substrate and filtration

Use planted tank substrate for the best results. Include driftwood, rocks, and caves to create natural hiding places.

Use gentle filtration systems like sponge filters or pre-filtered canister filters to avoid strong currents that may stress shrimps.

Shrimps prefer slow-moving water. Position the filter outlet or use a spray bar to disperse water gently across the tank.
