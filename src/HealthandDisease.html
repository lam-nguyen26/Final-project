Health and Disease

Let's learn more about shrimps and aquariums

Common health issues

- Molting Problems: Shrimps shed their exoskeletons to grow. Molting issues, such as unsuccessful molts or molting problems due to calcium deficiency

- Bacterial Infections: Particularly from poor water quality or injuries, can cause visible discoloration or lethargy

- Parasitic Infections: Parasites like worms, protozoans, or crustacean diseases can affect shrimp health, causing symptoms like lethargy, abnormal behavior

Treatment

- The best treatment is to change the water and use commercial medication. Also, quarantine new additions before introducing them to the main tank to prevent diseases
