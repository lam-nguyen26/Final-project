Species Profile

Let's learn more about shrimps and aquariums

Neocaridina shrimps

Neocaridinas are the hardiest of freshwater dwarf shrimp. They prefer hard water and are very easy to breed. The water in Utah is already hard so it's perfect to keep them in Utah. Also, people usually don't breed different colors of shrimps together because they will crossbreed and lose that color. Within one year of crossbreeding, the color can go back to the natural color which is brown

Caridina shrimps

Caridina shrimps require more care and only suitable for someone who has experience. They also don't thrive well with Neocaridina shrimps because they prefer softer water. So it's best to keep Neocaridina shrimps and Caridina shrimps separately in different tanks.
