Care and Maintenance

Let's learn more about shrimps and aquariums

Care

- Aquatic shrimps are very adaptable and can live in a 1 gallon tank. But the ideal tank for them is at least 10 gallons with plenty of plants such as mosses, Java fern, and Anubias . Avoid using fake plants in shrimp tanks because those plants are boring and don't provide food for the shrimps. Plus live plants improve water quality and create grazing area for the shrimps. Their favorite food of all time is boiled zuchini.

Maintenance

Shrimp tanks don't need much maintenance or attention because shrimps are perfect tank cleaner and they will eat almost anything that is decaying or they even eat fish poop
