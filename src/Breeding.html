Breeding

Let's learn more about shrimps and aquariums

Overall

- There are 2 most important things that decide how fast they will breed: Nutrition and Water parameters

Nutrition

We need to feed them plenty of protein in order for the females to produce eggs. Ensure the diet includes a calcium source, as it is crucial for healthy molting and the development of shrimp exoskeletons.

Water parameters

Maintain stable and suitable water parameters. The best temperature for them to breed is somewhere between 72 degree to 18 degree. Don't change water too often or it will stress out the shrimps.
